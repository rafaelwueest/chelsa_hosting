Climatologies at high resolution for the earth’s land surface areas

Downloads (reduzed file sizes)

You are currently on the download-site hosted by the Swiss Federal Research institute for Forest, Snow and Landscape Research WSL. This site provides climate layers as produced by the CHELSA project.

Be aware that the climatic layers provided here are smaller in disk space than the original layers provided on the CHELSA download site. Compression of file-size is achieved by providing 2-byte integer layers:

  • Precipitation is rounded to integers and the provided unit is [mm].
  • Temperature layers are rounded to 0.1°C and provided in the unit [1/10°C].
  • Bioclimatic variables: see description for each layer

With the download of this data you acknowledge to cite the data using the following citation:

Karger, D.N., Conrad, O., Böhner, J., Kawohl, T., Kreft, H., Soria-Auza, R.W., Zimmermann, N., Linder, H.P. & Kessler, M. (2016) Climatologies at high resolution for the Earth land surface areas. arXiv:1607.00217 [physics].
